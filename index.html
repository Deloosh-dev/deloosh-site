Deloosh.

Email deliverability infrastructure.

Deloosh is the protective layer between a send and the inbox — delivering authenticated, permission-based transactional and lifecycle email on behalf of brand customers, and protecting every sender's reputation while it does.

What we do

  • Authenticated sending. Every customer sends from its own domain with DKIM, SPF, and DMARC alignment.
  • Per-tenant reputation isolation. Each brand is isolated, so one sender's behaviour never affects another's deliverability.
  • Real-time protection. We pace delivery against live bounce and complaint signals, suppress bad addresses automatically, and halt the moment reputation is at risk.
  • Permission-based, always. We send only to recipients with a direct relationship to the brand — never purchased, rented, or scraped lists.

Email & privacy practices

Every message carries a working unsubscribe, honoured immediately. Bounces and complaints are suppressed automatically and never re-contacted. We process recipient data solely to deliver mail on behalf of our customers and never sell it. Questions or requests: hello@deloosh.com.
