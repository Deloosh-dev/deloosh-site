What we do

Four guarantees standing between your mail and a spam folder.

Authenticated sending

Every customer sends from its own domain with DKIM, SPF, and DMARC alignment — so mailbox providers know exactly who's sending.

Per-tenant reputation isolation

Each brand is isolated, so one sender's behaviour never affects another's deliverability. No noisy neighbours, ever.

Real-time protection

We pace delivery against live bounce and complaint signals, suppress bad addresses automatically, and halt the moment reputation is at risk.

Permission-based, always

We send only to recipients with a direct relationship to the brand — never purchased, rented, or scraped lists.

Email & privacy practices

Respect for the recipient is built into the pipeline.

Questions or requests

A working unsubscribe on every message, honoured immediately — no exceptions, no delays.

Bounces and complaints are suppressed automatically and never re-contacted.

We process recipient data solely to deliver mail on behalf of our customers — and never sell it.
